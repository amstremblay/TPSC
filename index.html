Welcome to TPSC’s documentation!

TPSC is a Python library that allows the computation of Hubbard model related functions and quantities (such as the self-energy and Green’s function) using the Two-Particle-Self-Consistent (TPSC) approach first described in [Vilk and Tremblay, 1997].

TPSC is developped at the Institut Quantique of Sherbrooke University (Quebec, Canada) by students of André-Marie Tremblay group. It aims to provide a ready-to-use implementation of the TPSC method and some functions to post-process the results of the calculation.
